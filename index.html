Our Heritage
"Rooted in the industrial heart of Sangli district, we transform raw kraft paper into the sturdy shields that carry your business forward across India."

Based in Malgaon, Miraj, Om Packaging Industries has been a trusted name in corrugated box manufacturing — serving Maharashtra's agricultural belts, e-commerce sellers, and industrial clients. We blend traditional manufacturing reliability with modern precision, offering custom sizing, multi-ply strength options, and branded printing under one roof. Whether you're a small Flipkart seller or a large-scale agricultural exporter, we scale with your needs.
